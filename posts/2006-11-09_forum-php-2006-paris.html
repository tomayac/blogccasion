Forum PHP 2006 Paris
Today I was talking at the Forum PHP 2006 in Paris which is organized by the Association of French PHP Users (AFUP). My presentation was titled 'Ajax and Web Services in PHP - Google AdWords API with APIlity'.
It included live demos of APIlity, APIlitAx, and of a Mashup of Google Checkout and AdWords, based on the presentation at the Mashup Camp that I gave earlier in July. The slides are available in PDF format.

Links for this presentation:
  • APIlity (A PHP library for accessing the Google AdWords API)
  • APIlitAx (An Ajax GUI for Google AdWords)
