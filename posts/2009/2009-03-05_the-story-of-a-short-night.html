Well, as you have read, the story's ending (or should I say its beginning?) has been different. The doctors were convinced that the baby would not come that night, and suggested to get some sleep for the events to come. Well, as it was quite late, I did not catch the last subway, so I had to wait some time at a subway station to catch at least one of the night service busses. As I was really tired I fell asleep immediately after having arrived at home by 1:30 in the middle of the night. Exactly 2.5 hours of sleep later, Laura called telling me that the contractions had started.
