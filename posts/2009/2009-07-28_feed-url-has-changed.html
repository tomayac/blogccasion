My ATOM feed URL has changed. It is way shorter now, the dynamic parameters have been removed, and now I use the feed as a Sitemap as well. Using RSS or ATOM feeds as Sitemap is a not so well known chapter of the Sitemap story. I will leave the old URL active for a while, but you are highly recommended to update your feed reader to include the new URL https://blog.tomayac.com/atom.php. Thanks, and sorry for the inconvenience.

At some point I want to restructure the whole blog as well, starting with the feed URL is a first step. Looking forward to the next one. Sometime.
