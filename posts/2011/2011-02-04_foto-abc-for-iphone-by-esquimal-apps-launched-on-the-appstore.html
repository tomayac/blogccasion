My wife Laura is super proud of her very first iPhone app Foto ABC that went live on the AppStore on February 1, 2011 (and I'm obviously very proud of her). The app is for learning the alphabet and is localized in five languages: English, French, Spanish, German, and Catalan, Laura's native language. She put a lot of work into the selection of really nice photos, all Creative Commons licensed.
