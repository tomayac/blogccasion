Dear APIlity users,

Finally APIlity 1.4.4 can be downloaded.

For details please view the complete ChangeLog.

This release introduces support for API v4, and therefore supports
  • local timezones
  • traffic estimator ranges for avgPosition, clicksPerDay and cpc

Please note the parameter changes in the getXmlReport() and getCsvReport() functions. Additionally, the date format for
  • Stats (getCriterionStats(), getCampaignStats(), getAdGroupStats())
  • Reports (getXmlReport(), getCsvReport())
  • Campaign start/end dates ($campaignObject->setEndDate(), addCampaign(), addCampaignList(), addCampaignsOneByOne())
has been changed.
Hope you like the new release.

PS: Feel free to subscribe to the APIlity announcement group: https://groups.google.com/group/APIlity-Announce?lnk=li.
