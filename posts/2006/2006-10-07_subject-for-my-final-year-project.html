Slowly I am heading to the last period of my studies: the final year project lasting six months. Now I have selected a subject: the working title is 'Automatic multi-language program library generation for REST APIs' ('Automatische Programmbibliotheksgenerierung in mehreren Programmiersprachen für REST-APIs'). The project will be an external thesis with Google, supervised by Prof. Calmet from the University of Karlsruhe, and Catherine Oriat from the ENSIMAG in Grenoble. I am really looking forward to February 5, 2007, where a new era will begin. Not only because of the start of the final year project, but also because of my relocation to Barcelona. For some wonderful reason called love…
