REST Describe & Compile is currently in feature freeze state for the upcoming presentation of the project in France at the ENSIMAG university. I have taken the current version 0.3 for the presentation as it represents code generation for both dynamically and statically typed languages and should be stable enough... Hopefully. You can check my ongoing progress on the project report almost in real time by pointing your browser to the published Google document.

In addition to that there is another article about the application available online. Stefan Tilkov from InfoQ has published a story including a quick email interview. Thank you for your interest. The article also unveils that after more than two years of part-time work for Google during my studies, I have finally signed a full-time contract with Google.
